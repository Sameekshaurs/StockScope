About StockScope

StockScope is an AI-powered platform designed to simplify stock market analysis for learners, students, and aspiring investors. It provides a simplified and educational environment where anyone can explore how markets behave, understand prediction models, and build financial literacy through data-driven learning.

With customizable stock inputs, interactive visualizations, and machine learning–powered forecasting, StockScope bridges the gap between technology and finance. The system uses live market data to deliver accurate insights and forecasts, empowering users to analyze trends with ease. Built with a secure login system and accessible on all devices, it offers a modern, seamless, and user-friendly experience for everyone interested in understanding the world of stocks.
